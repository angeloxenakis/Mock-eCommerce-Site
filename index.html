Our Heritage Process

When making our products we have three goals in mind; precise design, durability, and easy to repair. We take numerous steps to ensure that your product will be perfect when it arrives, last a long time, and if something happens we can repair it quickly. We aren't afraid of giving out our trade secrets. We believe that these steps are necessary to making a great product. We want to eliminate waste and ensure longevity of a product. That alone will help reduce the consumption of products that should rarely be replaced and decrease the effect of what over production of these products have on the environment. We start by purchasing leather hides from the highest quality tanneries in the US. Purchasing hides from companies that care about quality just as much as we do is key. Once we have received the leather we inspect every piece to make sure you are getting truly the best of the best.
